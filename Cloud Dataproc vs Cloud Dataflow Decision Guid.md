### **Choose [[Cloud Dataproc]] if:**

- You have dependencies on Apache Hadoop or Spark ecosystem tools/packages
- You prefer a hands-on/DevOps approach to operations
- You want to migrate existing Spark/Hadoop workloads

### **Choose [[Cloud Dataflow]] if:**

- No specific Hadoop/Spark dependencies
- You prefer a hands-off/serverless approach
- You want Google to handle all infrastructure management
